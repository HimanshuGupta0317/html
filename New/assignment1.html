My Favorite BooK:IGNITED MIND



About the Book

Unleashing the Power Within India is a book written by Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, President of India from 2002 to 2007. Dr. Kalam dedicated Ignited Minds to an intermediate school child he met at a school. While talking to students, a question came up: “Who is our enemy?” This book is inspiring and full of hope for India.

Why I Love It

Each one of us must become aware of our higher self. We are links of a great past to a grand future. We should ignite our dormant inner energy and let it guide our lives. The radiance of such minds embarked on constructive endeavour will bring peace, prosperity and bliss to this nation.
